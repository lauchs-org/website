Resources

Listed below are some useful resources for your schoolwork and extracurricular activities. Note that we are not affiliated with or endorsed by any of the organizations, products, and brands featured on this list.

Microsoft Office 365 Education

Teachers and students can sign up for Microsoft Office 365 Education, which includes 1TB of cloud storage, for free here. Make sure you use your school email address (i.e. username@lauchs.org) in order to qualify for the offer.

Education discounts are available to students for other software, such as Minecraft Education and desktop Office apps. Families interested in purchasing these products can contact us for more details.

Canva for Education

Canva is graphic design platform that provides students and teachers tools and templates to easily create professional-looking presentations, posters, and other graphics. To gain free access to additional features available to the education plan, contact us so we can add you to our school team.
