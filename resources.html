Resources

Listed below are some useful resources for your schoolwork and extracurricular activities. Note that we are not affiliated with or endorsed by any of the organizations, products, and brands featured on this list.

Canva for Education

Canva is graphic design platform that provides students and teachers tools and templates to easily create professional-looking presentations, posters, and other graphics. To gain free access to additional features available to the education plan, contact us so we can add you to our school team.

NIH STEM Teaching Resources

Free K-12 STEM education materials provided by the National Institutes of Health (NIH). Topics focus on human health, environmental science, and scientific tools & methods.

Internet2 Community Anchor Program

The Internet2 Community Anchor Program (CAP) supports teachers to use the internet to teach in new and exciting ways and open up the world to their students. A list of external resources contains some of CAP’s favorite online and distance learning resources for K-12 students everywhere.

iD Tech Scholarship Programs

Through individual applications, corporate sponsorships, nonprofit partners and schools, iD Tech has introduced students throughout the country to STEM, sparking their curiosity in tech.

Google CS First

CS First empowers every teacher to teach computer science with tools and resources that are free of charge.
